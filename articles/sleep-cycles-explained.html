What Are Sleep Cycles and Why Do They Matter?

Your body doesn’t sleep in one long phase. Instead, you move through repeating 90-minute cycles. Knowing this can drastically improve how rested you feel.

1. The Four Stages of Sleep

  • Stage 1: Light sleep, drifting.
  • Stage 2: Deeper relaxation, slower heart rate.
  • Stage 3: Deep sleep — physical recovery.
  • REM Sleep: Dreaming, memory processing.

2. Why Cycles Matter

Waking up in the middle of a cycle = groggy and tired.
Waking up between cycles = refreshed and energized.

3. The 90-Minute Rule

Each cycle averages 90 minutes. Multiplying cycles helps you plan better sleep:

  • 4 cycles → 6 hours
  • 5 cycles → 7.5 hours
  • 6 cycles → 9 hours
