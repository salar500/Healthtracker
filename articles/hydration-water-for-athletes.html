How Much Water Do Athletes Need Daily?

Athletes and active individuals need significantly more water due to sweating, higher metabolism, and muscle activity.

1. Baseline Water Needs

The average person needs around 30–35 ml per kg of body weight. Athletes exceed this.

2. Add Water for Every Hour of Exercise

Add **0.5 to 1 liter** of water per hour of intense exercise.

3. Hydrate Before, During, and After Activity

  • Before: 500 ml water
  • During: 150–250 ml every 15–20 minutes
  • After: Replace fluids lost through sweat

4. Check Sweat Loss

Weigh yourself before and after exercise. Each 1 kg lost = 1 liter water needed.

5. Include Electrolytes

During long workouts, drink fluids containing sodium, potassium, and magnesium.
