How Much Water Do Athletes Need Daily?

Athletes and active individuals require more water due to sweating, increased metabolism, and muscle activity.

1. Baseline Water Requirements

The general recommendation is 30–35 ml per kg of body weight. Athletes typically require more due to higher activity levels.

2. Extra Water for Exercise

Add 0.5 to 1 liter of water for every hour of intense exercise or sports.

3. Hydrate Before, During & After Workouts

  • Before: 500 ml of water
  • During: 150–250 ml every 15–20 minutes
  • After: Replace fluids lost through sweat

4. Monitor Sweat Loss

Weigh yourself before and after workouts. Each 1 kg lost = 1 liter of water needed to rehydrate fully.

5. Include Electrolytes

During extended training sessions, add fluids containing sodium, potassium, and magnesium to prevent cramps and fatigue.
