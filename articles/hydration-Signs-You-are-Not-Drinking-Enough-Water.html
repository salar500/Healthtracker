Signs You're Not Drinking Enough Water

Dehydration affects your body faster than most people realize. These are the most common signs that you may not be drinking enough water.

1. Dark Yellow Urine

Healthy urine should be pale and straw-colored. Darker shades are a clear signal of dehydration.

2. Persistent Headaches

Even mild dehydration can trigger headaches and difficulty concentrating.

3. Dry Mouth & Bad Breath

Low saliva production allows bacteria to grow, causing dryness and unpleasant breath.

4. Fatigue & Low Energy

Water is essential for energy production and circulation. Low intake leads to tiredness.

5. Dizziness or Lightheadedness

Dehydration can temporarily reduce blood pressure, which may cause dizziness.
