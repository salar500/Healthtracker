What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a simple calculation used to estimate whether a person’s weight is healthy for their height.

How BMI is Calculated

The formula is:

BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²)

Why BMI is Used

BMI is widely used because it is simple, fast, and useful for identifying general weight categories.

Limitations

BMI does not measure muscle mass, fat distribution, or overall health — it is only a screening tool.
