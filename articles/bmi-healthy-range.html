BMI Healthy Ranges

BMI categories help identify whether an adult’s weight is within a healthy range.

Standard BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5: Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9: Healthy / Normal Weight
  • 25 – 29.9: Overweight
  • 30 and above: Obesity

These categories are general guidelines. A full health evaluation requires more than BMI alone.
