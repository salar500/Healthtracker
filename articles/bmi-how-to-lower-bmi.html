How to Lower Your BMI Safely

Lowering your BMI doesn’t require extreme dieting. Instead, sustainable lifestyle changes can make a lasting difference.

1. Increase Daily Activity

Walking at least 30 minutes per day improves metabolism and helps regulate weight.

2. Choose High-Volume, Low-Calorie Foods

Vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains help reduce calories without leaving you hungry.

3. Cut Back on Liquid Calories

Sugary drinks, juices, and sodas add a large number of calories without filling you up.

4. Improve Sleep Quality

Poor sleep increases hunger hormones, making weight control harder.

5. Drink More Water

Staying hydrated helps control appetite and improve metabolism.
