How to Lower Your BMI Safely

Lowering your BMI doesn’t require extreme dieting. Instead, sustainable lifestyle changes can make a lasting difference.

1. Increase Daily Activity

Walking at least 30 minutes per day improves metabolism and helps regulate weight.

2. Choose High-Volume, Low-Calorie Foods

Vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, and whole grains reduce calorie intake without leaving you hungry.

3. Cut Back on Liquid Calories

Sodas, juices, and sugary drinks add large amounts of calories without filling you up.

4. Improve Sleep Quality

Poor sleep disrupts hunger hormones, making weight management more difficult.

5. Drink More Water

Staying hydrated helps control appetite and supports healthy metabolism.
