How to Lower Your BMI Safely

Lowering your BMI doesn’t have to involve extreme diets or unhealthy routines. Here are proven and safe ways to lower BMI based on medical recommendations.

1. Increase Daily Movement

Walking 30 minutes daily can significantly improve BMI and overall fitness.

2. Eat High-Volume, Low-Calorie Foods

Foods like vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and lean proteins help reduce calorie intake.

3. Avoid Liquid Calories

Sodas, juices, and sugary drinks contribute massively to weight gain.

4. Improve Sleep Quality

Poor sleep increases hunger hormones and leads to weight gain.

5. Stay Hydrated

Water boosts metabolism and reduces overeating caused by thirst mistaken for hunger.
