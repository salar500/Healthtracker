Signs You're Not Drinking Enough Water

Dehydration can affect your body more quickly than you might think. Here are the most common symptoms that signal you need more water.

1. Dark Yellow Urine

Light straw-colored urine is ideal. Darker shades indicate dehydration.

2. Persistent Headaches

Even mild dehydration can trigger headaches or difficulty concentrating.

3. Dry Mouth or Bad Breath

Lack of saliva causes bacterial growth, leading to dryness and odor.

4. Fatigue and Low Energy

Water is essential for circulation and oxygen flow. Lower intake = more fatigue.

5. Dizziness or Lightheadedness

Dehydration can temporarily reduce blood pressure, causing dizziness.
