About HealthTracker

HealthTracker was created with a simple goal — to make health and fitness tracking tools accessible, accurate, and completely private.

Our Philosophy

Good design is invisible — it simply works. Every calculator runs locally in your browser with no data stored, no ads, and no distractions.

Our Tools

From BMI to hydration and sleep calculators, we build accurate, simple utilities for everyday wellness — designed for people, not data mining.

Our Vision

We want a world where anyone can understand their wellness effortlessly — no sign-ups, no complex apps. Just clean, transparent tools you can trust.
