About HealthTracker

HealthTracker was created with a simple goal — to make health and fitness tracking tools accessible, accurate, and completely private.

Our Philosophy

We believe good design is invisible — it simply works. That’s why HealthTracker focuses on clarity and function. Every calculator and tool we make runs directly in your browser with no data stored, no ads, and no distractions. You get results instantly — private, fast, and reliable.

Our Tools

From simple BMI and hydration calculators to sleep and calorie estimators, we build accurate utilities for everyday health awareness. Each tool is tested for usability and precision — designed for people, not data collection.

Our Vision

We’re building a world where anyone can understand their wellness through simple, transparent technology. No sign-ups, no complex apps — just clean tools you can trust. HealthTracker aims to expand into a full suite of calculators that make health knowledge open and effortless.

“Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.”

— Inspired by Steve Jobs’ philosophy of simplicity and function
