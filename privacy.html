Privacy Policy

At HealthTracker, your privacy is our top priority. We believe you should have complete control over your data. That’s why all our tools work directly in your browser — we never collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. No Data Collection

HealthTracker does not collect, track, or store any personal data. Every calculation — whether BMI, hydration, or sleep — happens entirely in your browser. We don’t use cookies, analytics, or databases to store user inputs.

2. No Third-Party Tracking

We don’t allow third-party tracking scripts or ad networks. Our site does not use any marketing pixels, user profiling systems, or cross-site tracking.

3. Open & Transparent Code

All calculations on HealthTracker are performed with open, client-side JavaScript. You can inspect the code anytime to verify our privacy claims. Transparency and trust are core to our design philosophy.

4. External Links

Our tools may contain links to external health or educational resources for reference. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

5. Security

Since no data is collected, the risk of data breaches is practically eliminated. Still, we ensure that our site uses HTTPS encryption to provide a secure connection every time you visit.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements in our services or new regulations. Any updates will be clearly posted on this page with the revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us anytime at Contact Page.
