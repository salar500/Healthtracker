Privacy Policy

At HealthTracker, your privacy is our top priority.
Our tools run entirely in your browser — we never collect or store personal data.

1. No Data Collection

HealthTracker does not collect, track, or store personal data. All calculators — BMI, Hydration, and Sleep — operate locally in your browser.

2. No Third-Party Tracking

We include no analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies, and no advertising scripts. No profiling. No data sharing. No surveillance of any kind.

3. Transparent Code

All calculation logic is handled by simple, client-side JavaScript. Users can review the code to verify our privacy-first design.

4. External Links

Our site may include links to external sources. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites.

5. Security

Since we do not store any data, risk is minimal. Still, we use secure HTTPS protection across our entire website.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may update occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, visit our Contact Page.
